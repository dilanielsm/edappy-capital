What is Edappy?

Edappy is a fully featured learning management system that seamlessly integrates course and learner management, content creation and publishing.Â  Its unique content engine allows academic and professional authors to write once and publish to a fully responsive virtual learning environment where learners can access their courses from their smartphones, tablets, laptops and PC's.

Our Experience

In developing Edappy, we have worked for years with teams of authors, tutors and students, as well as awarding bodies and teaching organisations. Through this we have gained the experience to develop a platform that reflects the needs of these stakeholders in delivering and receiving online learning programmes.

Our Technology

Edappy is a cloud based, multi-tenanted platform built using a microservices architecture. This gives many benefits in terms of rapid development and deployment of services and features, software maintenance, cost, security and scalability.

Our Users

Edappy is working with individual instructors, small and large teaching organisations and awarding bodies in the UK, Middle East, Asia and Africa to bring a professional mobile learning experience to learners around the world.
