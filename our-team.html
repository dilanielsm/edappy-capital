Meet the Team

Edappy are looking for enthusiastic and driven software developers to join our team in Cork and help build the leading teaching and learning platform in the cloud.

We will take your desire to innovate and grow your knowledge of leading technologies, give you the tools and environment to flourish and provide a competitive salary package. As a fast-growing startup, we want to hear from developers of all experience levels with a thirst for learning and a will to influence how we build our platform. If you are experienced in some of these skills, or can show you are hungry to master them, please apply now to jobs@edappy.com.

  • JavaScript - enterprise grade
  • AngularJS
  • MongoDB, Redis or other SQL and NoSQL databases
  • AWS: EC2, S3, ECS, CloudFront
  • Microservices architectures
  • Docker, Consul, Mesos or other service discovery and distributed cluster
    management platforms
  • Evolving DevOps best practices
  • Continuous integration and deployment
  • Obsessive about quality and continuous improvement

We are also interested in experience you have in any other technologies. We value diversity.
